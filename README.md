## About

I'm an Enterprise Security Architect at Booz Allen Hamilton, supporting the firm's CISO on product security. That means security reviews, hardening, and making sure R&D teams are building things securely from the start (FedRAMP, CMMC, SSDF, Secure-by-Design). Before this I spent over a decade on DHS CISA's CDM program, working from solution architect up to running a 150+ person engineering organization across multiple federal agencies.

I'm also pursuing a PhD at George Mason in Systems Engineering & Operations Research. My research applies causal AI to predicting when large-scale cyber defense implementations are about to go sideways, built on what I've seen go wrong (and right) on CDM over the years.
